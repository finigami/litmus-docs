## Scheduling suite runs  
![Scheduling runs](/images/running/schedule-new.png)
1. Open the suite and select the **Schedules** tab.  
2. Click **Add Schedule**.  
3. Choose hourly or daily recurrence. 
4. Select the **Environment**.and **Config** .
5. Click **Add**.

![Scheduling runs](/images/running/schedule-view.png)
> **Note:** All schedules for that suite will appear in this view
